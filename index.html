Welcome to Janes Pilates studio.

Jane has been teaching Pilates for over 5 years. As a former nurse, she is focused on using pilates as a means to help recover from injury, and to mentain physical and mental health.

Our studio caters for all experience levels, from beginners, intermediate to advanced. We hold classes daily, as well as private individual or group bookings.

We are located in the N5 industrial estate on the Breaffy road, just outside Castlebar, Co Mayo

Pilates is thought to have several benefits such as:

  1. Back pain
  2. Relieve tension in your shoulders, back and legs
  3. Boosts your body's natural ability to burn fat
  4. Prevents injury
  5. Promotes mobility
  6. Improves strength
  7. Promotes mindfullness
